Simple success-based pricing

Pricing

RefundRadar is free to install and free to use until you confirm that a retailer issued a refund or credit.

Success Fee

27%

of the refund or credit amount you confirm receiving.

  • No monthly subscription in the current plan.
  • No charge when a retailer denies the claim.
  • No charge just for detecting a purchase or finding a potential drop.
  • Payments are processed through Paddle checkout.
Example: if you confirm a $100.00 retailer refund, RefundRadar’s success fee is $27.00. Scanning pauses until the Paddle payment is completed.

How payment works

When you confirm a successful refund, RefundRadar calculates the success fee server-side, locks background scanning, and opens Paddle checkout for the calculated amount. After a verified payment success event is received, your license returns to active and scanning resumes.

Taxes and payment processor

Paddle processes payments and may calculate applicable taxes depending on your location. Paddle may appear on your payment statement.
