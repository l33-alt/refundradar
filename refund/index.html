Refund Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Retailer refunds

Retailer refunds or credits are issued by the retailer, not RefundRadar. RefundRadar helps identify policy-backed opportunities and prepare claim information, but the retailer makes the final approval decision.

RefundRadar success fee refunds

RefundRadar charges only after you confirm receiving a refund or credit. If a success fee is charged by mistake, or if the confirmed refund amount was entered incorrectly, contact support within 30 days.

When we refund our fee

  • The fee was calculated from the wrong confirmed refund amount.
  • The same success fee was charged more than once.
  • A verified Paddle processing error caused an incorrect charge.
  • Support determines the retailer reversed the refund shortly after confirmation.

When our fee is not refundable

  • You accurately confirmed a refund or credit and the fee was correctly calculated.
  • You later choose not to use the refunded funds or store credit.
  • The request is made after the review period without evidence of billing error.

How to request a fee refund

Email officialdhital33@gmail.com with your account email, retailer, order date, confirmed refund amount, and Paddle payment receipt. Approved fee refunds are returned through Paddle to the original payment method.
