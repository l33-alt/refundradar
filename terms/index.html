1. Service

RefundRadar monitors supported retailer purchases for policy-backed price adjustment opportunities and helps you prepare claim requests. Retailers make the final decision on whether to issue a refund or credit.

2. Success fee

RefundRadar charges a success fee equal to 27% of the refund or credit amount you confirm receiving. No fee is due if a retailer denies the claim or if no refund/credit is issued.

3. Paddle checkout and license lock

If you confirm a successful refund, RefundRadar may lock your license state, pause background scanning, and present a Paddle checkout for the calculated success fee. Scanning resumes after a verified payment success event.

4. User responsibilities

  • Provide accurate order and refund information.
  • Submit claims only for purchases you made.
  • Do not abuse referrals, chargebacks, or policy windows.
  • Keep account credentials secure.

5. No guaranteed retailer outcome

RefundRadar cannot guarantee a retailer will approve a claim. We improve eligibility by focusing on documented policies and evidence, but retailer approval is outside our control.

6. Limitation of liability

RefundRadar is provided as-is. To the maximum extent permitted by law, our liability is limited to the fees you paid to RefundRadar in the twelve months before the claim.
