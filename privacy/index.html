Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Plain-English summary

RefundRadar collects only the data needed to track supported purchases and prepare policy-backed refund claims. We do not collect payment card numbers, CVV codes, passwords, browsing history, bookmarks, cookies, or keystrokes.

Payments are processed by Paddle. RefundRadar does not store full card numbers or CVV codes.

Security

Sensitive order fields are encrypted before storage. API calls use HTTPS. Access is authenticated.

Data sharing

We use service providers such as Supabase, hosting providers, Paddle, PostHog, Sentry, and email/notification providers. We do not sell individual user data.
